The Tenents of the Ministry

Our Beliefs

One (1) True God

We believe in the existence of the One True God, Elohim, Maker of the whole universe; indefinable, but revealed as Triune God– Father, Son and Holy Spirit- One in nature, essence, and attributes; Omniscient, Omnipotent and Omnipresent (Genesis 1: 1; Matthew 3: 16-17; 28:19; 2 Corinthians 13: 14).

Repentance, Justification, & Sanctification

We believe that all men must repent of, confess their sins before God, and believe in the death of Jesus Christ to be justified before God. We believe in the sanctification of the believer through the workings of the Holy Spirit and in God's gifts of eternal life to the believer. (Luke 15: 7; Acts 2: 38, Romans 4:25, Romans 5:16; 1 Corinthians 1: 30; 1 Thessalonians 4: 3).

The Bible

We believe in the divine inspiration and authority of the Holy Bible. We believe that the Bible is infallible in its declaration, final in its authority, all sufficient in its provisions and comprehensive in its sufficiency (2 Timothy 3: 16; 2 Peter 1: 21).

The Savior

We believe that man needs a Savior. This need has been met in the person of Jesus Christ for the following reasons: His deity, virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, resurrection, and ascension; also, His abiding intercession and second coming to judge the living and the dead (Matt 1: 21; John 4:42; Ephesians 5: 23; Phil 2: 6-11).

Baptism, Gifts, & Fruit of the HolySpirit

We believe that baptism in the Holy Spirit with speaking in tongues as initial evidence is for believers. Likewise, we believe in the operation of the gifts of the Holy Spirit and the bearing of fruits in the lives of believers (Mark 16: 17; Acts 2:4; 1 Cor. 12: 8-11; Gal. 5: 22-23).

Man's Depraved Nature

We believe that “all men have sinned and come short of the glory of God” and are subject to eternal punishment; and need repentance and regeneration (Genesis 3:1-19; Isaiah 53:6).

Sermon from: Pastor Daniel Mensah Categories: God, Pray On Sunday 13 Dec, 2019

John 10:35 Says, "If He called them gods, to whom the word of God came (and the scripture cannot be broken)" Also, 1 Peter 1:25 Says, "But the word of the Lord endures forever." This means that, the Word of God stands forever, provided we can work it. No matter what, Gods word will have the LAST SAY or LAST WORD in your case. Please wrap up your heart, your mind in this Truth and know that He who promised is also faithful, and He will watch over His word to perform it. Psalm 119:89 says, "Forever O Lord, Your word is settled in heaven." This means heaven approves the word. Heavens agrees to the Word. The word becomes the standard measurement or the final authority in its decision. The doctor or the lawyer does not have the final word. No matter what you are going through, it is not over yet. God's word will prevail as you trust in the word. Please rejoice that you have a word of Promise from Gods word.

About Open Valley Ministry

Founded in 2017, Open Valley Ministry (OVM) is nondenominational, multicultural ministry led by Senior Pastor, Daniel and Magdalene A. Mensah. As an Intercessor, Daniel began praying for the States within United States as well as countries around the world. During the early part of 2016, as part of his intercessory routine, the Spirit of the Lord laid upon his heart to begin interceding for Corpus Christi and other twelve surrounding cities. The passion of prayer for the city grew even greater, when he realized Corpus Christi was among the top Five–Crime Prone Cities in Texas (During that time). Daniel flew many times to the City and in his Hotel room, he spent ten to twelve hours a day for three or four days fasting and praying for the City and the purpose of God for His life. Also, during one of his marathon prayers in Dallas, The Spirit of the Lord, impressed upon his heart to preach on the Name of Jesus even as he comes to Corpus Christi. Truthful to his Word, God opened job opportunity door for him and relocated with his wife to the City and began Ministry after five months.

Mission Statement

The Mandate of Open Valley Ministry (OVM) is to preach “The Name JESUS CHRIST” to mankind, so as to rescue those in captivity of darkness into the Kingdom of God; to bring hope and life to the destitute in our communities by way of providing humanitarian services to the needy, the homeless, and to pull those in the Valley of Dry Bones to a place of prominence.
